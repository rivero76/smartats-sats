The Problem

Hiring is broken
for candidates

The modern job market is ruthless. Qualified candidates are filtered out before a human ever reads their résumé.

  • 🔴
    ATS black boxes — 75% of résumés are discarded automatically due to keyword mismatches, not qualification gaps.
  • 🔴
    Generic advice — career coaches offer one-size-fits-all tips with no data behind them.
  • 🔴
    No skills roadmap — candidates know they're missing skills but don't know which ones matter for which roles.
  • 🔴
    Manual scouting — job discovery is exhausting, imprecise, and reactive.

Product

Built feature by feature,
validated at every step

SmartATS ships with a rigorous verification gate — no feature goes live without runtime evidence.

🎯

ATS Resume Scoring Live

AI scores any résumé against any job description on a 0–100% scale with skill-by-skill breakdown and missing skills list.

✨

Experience Enrichment Live

AI rewrites bullet points to surface relevant skills — user reviews and approves every change before it's saved.

🔍

Proactive Job Discovery Live

Background async scorer surfaces only high-fit (≥60%) jobs via push notifications. No more doomscrolling job boards.

🗺️

Upskilling Roadmap Live

AI generates a sequenced milestone plan to close skill gaps, with timeline tracking and curated learning resources.

🔗

LinkedIn Ingestion Live

Import your full profile — skills, experiences, education — with a merge utility and HITL review UI.

📈

CV Optimisation Score Live

Projects your ATS score improvement if you accept AI enrichment suggestions. Shows the ROI of acting on advice.

🔬

Resume Intelligence Suite Live

Four parallel AI lenses on every analysis — Format Audit detects ATS-breaking patterns, Geography Mode adapts format advice to your target country, Industry Lens flags missing vertical-specific sections, and Cultural Tone Advisor catches register mismatches.

🧠

Skill Profile Engine Live

AI classifies skills from your work history into career chapters with time-decay weighting. Skill context is injected into every ATS analysis — so your most recently used skills score highest.

Competitive Advantage

Our defensible moat

Anyone can wrap GPT. We've built operational governance that competitors can't copy quickly.

01

Schema-Locked AI

LLM outputs are constrained by JSON schema — no hallucinated skill gaps, no fabricated recommendations. Every insight is evidence-bound.

02

HITL by Design

Human-in-the-loop is a first-class product feature, not an afterthought. Users approve every AI change — building trust, not dependency.

03

Enterprise-Ready from Day 1

RLS, audit logs, BYOK, data residency, and RBAC multi-seat are in the architecture from the start — not bolted on later.

04

Full Observability Stack

Centralized logging, cost analytics, LLM governance protocol, and a structured eval gate ensure quality at scale.

05

Geography-Aware Scoring

Resume format advice adapts to the target country — photo rules, length norms, personal detail conventions, date formats. Eight markets supported at launch, with an extensible architecture for global expansion.

06

Skill Time-Decay Intelligence

The Skill Profile Engine weights recently used skills higher than dormant ones — producing ATS scores that reflect where a candidate actually is today, not where they were five years ago.

07

Multi-Market Architecture

Localized job APIs (JSearch, Adzuna) for US, UK, AU, NZ, BR. Multi-language support planned. Built for global expansion.

08

Career Continuity

Career Aspirations Tracker turns a point-in-time tool into a long-term platform — 6-month and 5-year gap analysis creates lasting retention.
