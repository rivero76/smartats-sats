FAQ

Questions? Answered.

How does SmartATS score my résumé?

We use a schema-locked AI pipeline that compares your résumé content against the job description. The score reflects skill coverage, experience alignment, and keyword relevance — with a full breakdown so you can see exactly why.

Does the AI change my résumé without asking?

Never. Every AI suggestion — whether it's enriching a bullet point or recommending a skill — is presented for your review first. You approve each change before anything is saved. You stay in full control.

What file formats does it support?

We support PDF, DOCX, and HTML résumés. Job descriptions can be pasted in as text or entered as a URL — we'll extract the content automatically.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored securely with row-level security. We never share your résumé or job data with third parties. Max plan users can bring their own API keys to further limit data exposure.

How does proactive job discovery work?

SmartATS runs background scoring against new job postings. You're only notified when a job scores ≥60% match with your profile. No spam — just high-fit opportunities delivered to you.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings and your data will remain accessible during the billing period.

What data can I see on the Free plan?

Free users see the ATS compatibility score, matched skills, missing skills, extraction warnings, and error details. Score sub-dimensions, CV Optimisation, AI prompts, raw model output, and token usage are available on Pro and Max respectively.

Where do I upgrade my plan?

Go to Settings → Your Plan. You'll see a side-by-side comparison of all tiers with pricing and feature lists. Billing is launching soon — click "Request early access" to get on the list.
